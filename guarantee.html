Guarantee, Refunds & Disclaimers

Last updated: November 28, 2025

1. Educational Purpose Only

PeerPrep Academy provides educational and test-preparation coaching for MCAT students. Nothing on this website or in any tutoring session should be interpreted as legal, medical, academic, admissions, psychological, or professional advice.

2. Score Improvement Guarantee (+10 Points)

Your +10 point improvement guarantee applies only if all of the following conditions are met:

  • You complete a minimum of 20 tutoring hours.
  • You follow the custom study plan created for you.
  • Your baseline score is documented through an official AAMC Full-Length exam taken before tutoring begins.
  • Your final score must also be an official AAMC result taken after completing at least 20 hours.
  • You attend all scheduled sessions (missed or no-show sessions do not count toward the 20 hours).
  • You submit proof of both your baseline and final AAMC scores.

If every requirement is satisfied and you do not achieve a 10-point increase, you are eligible for a refund equal to 10% of the tutoring hours purchased.

3. Refund & Cancellation Policy

3.1 Service-Response Refund Only

All payments to PeerPrep Academy are non-refundable, except in one specific case:

You are eligible for a full refund only if PeerPrep Academy fails to make initial contact within 72 hours of your purchase.

“Initial contact” includes:

  • An email sent to the address you provided
  • A text message
  • A phone call
  • An attempt to schedule your first session

If any of the above forms of contact occur within 72 hours, the purchase becomes fully non-refundable.

3.2 No Refunds After Contact

Once initial contact is made—regardless of whether you reply, delay, change your mind, fail to schedule, or discontinue studying—the purchase remains non-refundable.

3.3 No Refunds After Tutoring Begins

Once tutoring communication or planning begins (including scheduling, assessments, or sending resources), all payments are fully non-refundable.

3.4 Cancellations & No-Shows

  • Cancellations must be made at least 12 hours in advance.
  • Cancellations under 12 hours are charged as a completed session.
  • No-shows count as a completed session.

3.5 Rescheduling

  • Sessions may be rescheduled once per session without penalty.
  • Repeated same-day rescheduling may be charged as a full session.

3.6 Tutor Cancellations

If a tutor must cancel, you will be offered a rescheduled session or credited for the missed time. You will never be charged for tutor-initiated cancellations.

3.7 Expiration of Hours

  • Unused tutoring hours expire 18 months after purchase.
  • Expired hours have no cash value and are non-refundable.
